CHOCOLATE CAKE

INGREDIENTS

  • WHEAT FLOUR
  • COCOA POWDER
  • UNSALTED BUTTER
  • POWDERED SUGAR
  • WHIPPED CREAM
  • MILK
  • SWEETENED CONDENSED MILK
  • OIL

STEPS

  1. Mix the flour and the cocoa powder such that there is no lump.
  2. Keep some mixture aside for the frosting.
  3. Add the sweetened condensed milk to make the chocolate paste.
  4. Add some oil to the batter and some unsalted butter.
  5. Cover the oven bowl with butter paper ande greese the bottom with butter.
  6. Shift the batter in the microwave for 45 mins.
  7. Mix the already mixed chocolate powder with powdered sugar.
  8. Gradually add some milk till the chocolate sauce is ready.
  9. Remove the cake andthe chocolate sauce along with whipped cream.
